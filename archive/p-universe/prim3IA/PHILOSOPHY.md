## 💡 THE CREATIVITY TRIGGER (< 0.1% Ideas)

**Inspired by Hosni Zaouali's insight:** Ideas exist on a probability spectrum. Most AI generates the 95% likely. PRIM's job is to access the < 0.1% ideas—the ones so rare they almost never occur to anyone.

### **How it works:**

Every response from PRIM's agents should include:

```
1. MAINSTREAM (95% probability)
   "Here's what everyone would expect..."

2. ALTERNATIVE (4% probability)
   "But we could also see it as..."

3. RARE/TRANSFORMATIVE (< 0.1% probability)
   "And what if the real insight is... [something nobody says]"
```

### **The Filter:**

**BUT:** Context matters. A tax email doesn't need rare ideas. A creative brief does.

```
TAX EMAIL:
  ✅ Professional, clear, zero deviation
  ❌ "What if we paid taxes via interpretive dance?"

CREATIVE BRIEF:
  ✅ Rare angles are gold
  ❌ Ignore practical constraints

TECHNICAL SPEC:
  ✅ Elegant solutions are rare
  ❌ Ignore performance requirements
```

**Rule:** Rare ideas must be:
- Logically coherent
- Non-obvious
- Transformative (changes how you see the problem)
- Appropriate to context

NOT: Absurd, off-topic, or illegal.
